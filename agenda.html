Workshop Agenda

At the end of the training you will have a deep understanding of how to write accessible websites, you will be able to reliably develop, test and solve common problems related to Accessibility in realm of HTML 5 and RIA.

  • Introduction, Agenda Sharing 15 Minutes
  • Session 1 - Introduction to Disability and Assistive Technologies 60 Minutes presentation file
  • Tea Break 15 Minutes
  • Session 2 - Inaccessible Websites 60 Minutes
  • NVDA Screen Reader Set up and Its experience on Good and Bad websites 30 Minutes
  • Lunch 60 Minutes
  • Session 3 - Principle 1 - Perceivable, Guideline 1.1-1.4 - Hands on Exercises 60 Minutes presentation file
  • Session 4 - Principle 2 - Operable, Guideline 2.1-2.4 - Hands on Exercises 30 Minutes presentation file
  • Tea Break 15 Minutes
  • Session 5 - Principle 2 - Operable, Continue.. 30 Minutes
  • Session 6 - Principle 3 - Understandable, Guideline 3.1-3.3 - Hands on Exercises 60 Minutes presentation file
  • Day Summary, Wrap up and Feedback 15 Minutes

Day 2

  • Review of Yesterday Content 15 Minutes

  • Tea Break 15 Minutes
  • Session 8 - HTML5, Javascript and ARIA 60 Minutes presentation file
  • Hands on Examples of WAI ARIA 30 Minutes
  • Lunch 60 Minutes
  • Workshop Exercise Part - 1 90 Minutes
  • Tea Break 15 Minutes
  • Workshop Exercise Part - 2 90 Minutes
